# GlideRead Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

GlideRead does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Storage

All user preferences (theme, reading mode, font scale, line height, intensity, enabled sites) are stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage.sync API. This data syncs across your Chrome browsers via your Google account — GlideRead has no access to your Google account and does not operate any external servers.

## Permissions

- **activeTab**: Used solely to process text content on the page you are currently viewing. No page data is read, recorded, or transmitted.
- **storage**: Used solely to persist your GlideRead settings locally.
- **scripting**: Used solely to inject GlideRead's reading enhancement scripts into web pages you have explicitly enabled.
- **Host permissions**: Requested at runtime only when you enable a specific site. GlideRead never requests blanket access to all websites.

## Third-Party Services

GlideRead loads the Caveat font from Google Fonts for its settings page UI. Google Fonts may collect standard web request data (IP address, browser user-agent) per Google's privacy policy. No other third-party services are used.

## Analytics & Tracking

GlideRead does not use any analytics, tracking, telemetry, or advertising services.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the update will be published with a new version of the extension.
